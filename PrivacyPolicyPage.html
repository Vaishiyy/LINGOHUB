At Language Learning App, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, email, age, and language preferences.
  • Usage Data: Progress, lesson completion, and interaction with features.
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, OS version, and app crashes.
  • Payment Data (if applicable): For premium subscriptions, processed securely via third-party providers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize your learning experience.
  • To improve app functionality and content.
  • To communicate updates, promotions, and support.
  • To analyze trends and prevent fraud.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • With Service Providers: For analytics, payments, and cloud storage.
  • For Legal Reasons: If required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Anonymized Data: For research or marketing (no personal identification).

Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your data.

Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Opt out of marketing emails.
  • Disable cookies (via browser settings).

Children's Privacy

We comply with COPPA and do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will notify users of significant changes.
